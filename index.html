What is a Drone?

Drones, also known as unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), are aircraft that can be remotely controlled or fly autonomously through software-controlled flight plans. They are equipped with various technologies, such as GPS, sensors, and cameras, making them versatile tools for a range of applications.

Common Uses of Drones

Aerial Photography

Drones equipped with high-quality cameras are widely used in photography and videography for capturing stunning aerial shots.

Surveillance

Security agencies and military forces use drones for surveillance to monitor areas without human intervention.

Agriculture

In agriculture, drones are used for monitoring crop health, spraying pesticides, and managing fields more efficiently.

Search and Rescue

Drones help in search and rescue missions, especially in hard-to-reach areas or disaster-struck regions.

Types of Drones according to the wing type

Multi-Rotor Drones

These are the most common drones used by hobbyists and professionals alike. They have multiple rotors, which allow them to hover and fly in any direction.

Fixed-Wing Drones

Unlike multi-rotor drones, these have a wing design similar to airplanes and can cover large distances with minimal energy use.

Single Rotor helicopter Drones

These drones have one large rotor and a smaller one on the tail to help with direction. They are similar to helicopters in design.

Fixed-wing hybrid VTOL drones

As the latest drone technology to be introduced, fixed-wing hybrid VTOL drones refer to fixed-wing aircraft that have been modified to take off and land vertically.

Types of drones according to their sizes

Very small drones (Nano)

The size of these drones can be as small as an insect and easily fit in the palm of your hand, with dimensions between 1 and 50 cm.

Small drones

Small drones they are slightly larger than micro drones, between 50 cm and 2 meters.

Medium drones

Medium-sized drones are larger and heavier than smaller drones, with dimensions exceeding 2 meters and weighing up to 200 kilograms.

Large drones

Large drones are comparable in size to smaller aircraft and are used mainly for military purposes such as surveillance and strategy.

Types of drones according to their abilities & equipment

Toy drones

Toy drones it is conspicuous in the toy department of supermarkets, come in various sizes and shapes, with or without cameras

Military drones

Military drones, the origin of drones, are a completely different drone category from civilian drones.

Photography/videography drones

There are different kinds of drone models of cameras drones that can record photos or videos in HD, Full HD, or 4K, with the camera mounted on a 2-axis or 3-axis nacelle for perfect image stability.

Racing drones

Competitive drone or FPV racing is generally used on private and strictly regulated land and consists of obstacle courses
